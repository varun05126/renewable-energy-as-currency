What is Renewable Energy?

Renewable energy comes from natural sources that are replenished constantly, such as sunlight, wind, and water. Unlike fossil fuels, renewable energy reduces carbon emissions and helps fight climate change.

Uses of Renewable Energy

  • Generating electricity from solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal sources
  • Powering homes, businesses, and industries
  • Running electric vehicles and reducing reliance on fossil fuels
  • Providing sustainable energy solutions for remote areas
